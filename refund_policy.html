Refund & Cancellation Policy of The Skytrails

The Skytrails Private Limited is committed to protecting the confidentiality and privacy of its clients. We take necessary measures to safeguard personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, modification, or disclosure. We may use and disclose clients' personal information for the primary purpose for which it's collected, and for other related secondary purposes, as authorized by the Privacy Act. Generally, personal information is used for:

  1. Conducting our business,
  2. Providing and marketing our services,
  3. Communicating with clients,
  4. Complying with legal obligations,
  5. Managing and improving our services.

Please note, The Skytrails Private Limited does not issue refunds for early service withdrawal under any circumstances.

Refund Policy:

  1. Refunds are based on the full-service fee paid, not just the amount paid.
  2. Eligibility for refunds requires payment of the complete service fee.
  3. Refunds are not available if the client’s profile becomes ineligible after immigration announcements.
  4. We have a zero-tolerance policy for chargebacks. Disputed payments deemed valid may lead to being blacklisted and barred from our services.
  5. Tax components included in the total invoice amount are non-refundable.
  6. Refunds for rejections by Immigration Authorities are processed within 15-30 working days upon receipt of a completed Online Refund Claim Form and a copy of the rejection letter.
  7. The Skytrails Private Limited is not responsible for delays caused by third-party services, nor for refunding fees paid to external Assessing Bodies or Immigration Authorities.
  8. Clients using online card services agree not to charge back without following our refund norms.
  9. Service charges are non-refundable if the client fails to comply with necessary requirements or withdraws their application.
  10. No refunds are issued for permit rejections based on non-attendance of interviews, failure of medicals, non-compliance with embassy requirements, insufficient funds, prior immigration law violations, or late submission of additional papers.
  11. Clients must provide all necessary documents within 30 days of registration. Failure to do so will void any claim for a refund.
  12. The Skytrails Private Limited is not responsible for the outcome of immigration assessments and has no control over the approval process.
  13. Payments made to government bodies and other organizations for assessments, health tests, etc., are non-refundable.
  14. Clients must inform us of any changes in personal circumstances that might affect their application. Clients must achieve the required scores in English or other language tests as applicable. Refunds or transfers of fees are not available if a client withdraws from the process after signing up.
  15. Any undisclosed criminal or insolvency cases against the client or their dependents will invalidate any refund claims.
  16. Clients are informed about average waiting times for permit classes and understand that extended processing times do not entitle them to refunds.
  17. The Skytrails Private Limited does not guarantee job placements abroad.
  18. In case of disputes, our liability will not exceed the consulting charges paid.
  19. Refunds are not issued if a Green Card/Permanent Residence is not granted due to cap limits.
  20. Service amounts include only the individual’s request, not extended services to family or children.
  21. All fees are quoted in Indian Rupees and are responsible for all fees and applicable taxes.
  22. We are a private company and do not have the authority to grant permits.
  23. Our agreements are based on trust and transparency. All terms are clearly stated with no hidden conditions.
  24. Clients' decision to choose our services is their own and not influenced by us.
  25. Governing Law: The Skytrails Private Limited operates in compliance with the laws of the Government of India. In the event of any disputes, they will fall under the jurisdiction of courts in Punjab, Chandigarh, and New Delhi, NCT.
  26. Force Majeure: We are not liable for delays or failures in service due to events beyond our control.
  27. Clients agree not to claim a refund irrespective of the service outcome.

For further details, please contact us at +91 93113-93114 or email us at info@theskytrails.com. Our representatives will assist you promptly.
