Privacy Policy of The Skytrails

The Skytrails, a premier recruitment and manpower company specialising in immigration, work permit, and Visa services, is committed to upholding the highest standards of confidentiality and privacy. We understand the sensitivity of your personal information and treat all client data with the utmost respect and discretion.

Confidentiality Assurance:

  1. Information Handling: Personal and business proprietary information provided to The Skytrails is maintained with strict confidentiality. Access to this information is limited to a need-to-know basis within our organization.
  2. Personal Information Usage: We collect personal details only when you voluntarily send us a message. This information is used exclusively by our trained personnel to perform the services you have engaged us for.
  3. Email Privacy: Your email address will be used solely for the purpose it was provided for. We do not use client emails for marketing, nor do we share them without explicit consent.
  4. Information Sharing: The only circumstance under which your information might be shared is with government agencies involved in your immigration process, such as immigration authorities in various countries, under strict confidentiality protocols.
